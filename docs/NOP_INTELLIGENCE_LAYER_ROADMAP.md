## Phase 0 – Internal Prototype (Q1–Q2 2024)

- **Objectives:** Validate social position logging, reputation heuristics, and AI-powered moderation.
- **Tech Milestones:** Supabase schema for `social_positions`, basic wallet connect, CSV-based fee calculator.
- **Go-to-Market:** Closed alpha with internal operators, weekly signal reviews, and manual burn tracking.

## Phase 1 – Clean Dashboard (Q3 2024)

- **Objectives:** Ship unified dashboard with Explore feed, Wallet view, Boosted Tasks, and burn transparency.
- **Tech Milestones:** React AppShell, modular cards, token burn widget, news + intel feeds.
- **Go-to-Market:** Soft launch to 200 invite-only traders, community office hours, groundwork for exchange convos.

## Phase 2 – Protocol Positions & Reputation (Q4 2024 – Q1 2025)

- **Objectives:** Production-grade `social_positions` + `reputation_scores`, intelligence-feed API, fee modeling.
- **Tech Milestones:** Tx-hash registration flow, Alpha Score recalculations, wallet summaries for fees and burns.
- **Go-to-Market:** Public beta, first partner dashboards, early data room for listing teams.

## Phase 3 – On-Chain Fee Routing & Treasury (H1 2025)

- **Objectives:** Route the 1% fee on-chain via routers/aggregators, automate burns, and track treasury payouts.
- **Tech Milestones:** Smart contracts for fee split, scheduled burn executor, transparent treasury dashboard.
- **Go-to-Market:** Co-marketed campaigns with DEX/CEX partners, regular burn announcements, ecosystem grants.

## Phase 4 – Governance & Multi-Chain Expansion (H2 2025)

- **Objectives:** Activate NOP staking, governance modules, and multi-chain adapters (Arbitrum, Base, Solana).
- **Tech Milestones:** Staking + delegation contracts, cross-chain reputation syncing, protocol-owned liquidity vaults.
- **Go-to-Market:** Listing-grade governance rollout, integration with portfolio trackers, expansion to institutional desks.
